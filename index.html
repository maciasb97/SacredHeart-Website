☦︎ — Glory to Jesus Christ! — ☦︎

Welcome to Sacred Heart Byzantine Catholic Church! We are a Ruthenian(Byzantine) rite parish located in suburbs of Livonia, Michigan. In addition, our parish is governed by the Eparchy of Parma, located in the midwestern part of the United States.

We are in full communion with the Pope, Rome, and all of the other churches around the globe within the Catholic Church. We share in the same faith as the other churches in communion, but we specifically follow according to our Byzantine traditions and practices.

☦︎ — All Saints Shrine — ☦︎

Are you seeking pilgrimage and a closer connection to the great saints of the church? Then look no further and plan a trip to All Saints Shrine!

Located directly inside the parish, you will find relics of these holy men and women, beautifully displayed in a place of pious tranquility for your oppourtunity to pray with these Christians who have gone before you to their eternal reward with Our Lord and Savior in Heaven.

Note: While not required, please consider a donation to support our work of bringing these witnesses to the awesome power of God to the world.
